## New Features

- Always, innate, and at-will spells from feat and species sources count as prepared in party totals.
- Party spell interface now tracks spells granted by feats and species, with dedicated buckets on member cards; characters with only feat or species spells now appear in the party view and synergy analysis.

## Changes

- Party and synergy windows size themselves to their content, and party lists sort alphabetically.
- Synergy tooltips are grouped by party member, and the spell level and saving throw distributions list which members contribute to each entry.
